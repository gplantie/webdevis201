What does the future of Portugal Look Like?

The loss was devastating for not only Portugal fans but also for Ronaldo fans. This being his last world cup really puts it into perpsective of how time has gone by.

Portugal have been blessed with talent thoughout the years with great figure like Eusebio and Luis Figo. However, Cristiano Ronaldo's impact to Portuguese soccer is one that will go down in history. With his world cup career coming to an end we turn to the younger players for the future.

With the likes of players like Rafeal Leão and Gonçalo Ramos that have scored a collective number of 5 goals in the tournament and 18 year old Antonio Silva, we can trust that their future is bright.
